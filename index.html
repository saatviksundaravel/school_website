campus life

Auditorium

Multipurpose Indoor Auditorium on par with International Standards

A spacious Multipurpose Indoor Auditorium on par with International Standards is built to play indoor games and it will be best utilised to conduct all major functions in our school. It consists of three indoor courts viz., Basketball, Shuttle and Volleyball. All the posts are movable posts. It covers an area of nearly 27,000 sq. feet and can accommodate 2500 persons at a time. It has a semi gallery in which 500 persons can be seated.

Tennis courts

YRTV Tennis Academy

It is started infront of the Junior Block with 5 clay courts to enable 10 players to play at a time. It covers an area of nearly 45000 sq. ft. Flood lights are provided for proper lighting to play the game during the night. In addition to this, it has a car parking area and rest room facilities inside for the visitors and separate room is built for the players to take rest. NIS trained coaches are appointed to train the players and this academy is also open to all school students in our town.

green campus

Green and clean campus

YRTV has a huge and green campus. Eco friendlyness is of top priority in our school. There are thousans of trees and plants inside our vast spread campus and every single tree and plant is extremely taken care for maintanence. The campus will look green and clean where ever we see. Any plastic products or covers are strictly prohibited inside the school campus and its severely punishable. The whole campus is under the survilence of CCTV camera for extra safety
